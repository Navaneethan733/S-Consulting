Who We Are

Built to Deliver
Not Just Advise

STACKLY was founded in 2015 with a single conviction: that the consulting industry had to stop delivering thick reports and start delivering real results. Our founders — seasoned executives from Fortune 500 boardrooms and global investment banks — set out to build a firm that stays accountable.

Today, STACKLY is a global management consulting firm spanning 30+ countries, with a team of 25+ elite consultants who have lived the challenges they now help solve. We don't parachute in with boilerplate frameworks — we earn our place at the table every single day.

Our Journey

A Decade of
Breaking Boundaries

2015

The Founding

STACKLY is founded in Salem, India by three former Fortune 500 executives with a mission to make enterprise consulting honest and results-driven.

2017

First $10M Engagement

Secured our landmark $10M corporate restructuring assignment with a leading South Asian manufacturing group — a turning point that cemented our reputation.

2019

Global Expansion

Opened regional offices in Dubai and Singapore, bringing our playbook to GCC and Southeast Asian enterprises navigating rapid market change.

2021

Digital Practice Launch

Launched our dedicated Digital Transformation practice, helping over 80 companies migrate to cloud-native infrastructure and AI-driven workflows within two years.
